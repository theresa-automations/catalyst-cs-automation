## Product Overview

The Catalyst Crux Gear System is a modular attachment ecosystem for Catalyst cases. It includes four accessory types that attach to the four corner attachment points built into compatible Catalyst cases. All Crux accessories can be combined and swapped.

## Installation Summary (All Accessories)

**General principle for all Crux accessories:**
1. Locate the four corner attachment points on the Catalyst case
2. Thread the lanyard, strap, or clip through the attachment point
3. Ensure the connection is fully seated (pull-test before use)
4. For carabiner-based accessories: ensure the gate is fully closed and locked

**Coiled Lanyard:**
- Provides a retractable tether that stretches when needed and retracts when not in use
- Prevents accidental drops while allowing normal phone use
- Attach carabiner to bag, belt loop, or anchor point; thread lanyard through case attachment point

**Carabiner Attachment:**
- Direct clip from case attachment point to bag, belt loop, or gear
- Gate must be fully closed and locked — do not leave gate in half-open position

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: How does the lanyard attach?**
> Thread the lanyard cord through one of the four attachment points at the corners of the Catalyst case. Secure the end according to the included hardware (button or loop closure). Pull-test before use.

**Q: What is the coiled lanyard for?**
> The coiled lanyard provides a retractable tether between your phone and an anchor point (bag, clip, wrist). It stretches to allow normal phone use and retracts to keep the tether tidy. It prevents the phone from hitting the ground if dropped.

**Q: Will Crux accessories work with non-Catalyst cases?**
> Yes, as long as the other case has secure, dedicated attachment loops or rings of similar size. The accessories are optimized for Catalyst cases' four attachment points.

**Q: How do I make sure the carabiner is safe?**
> The carabiner gate must be fully closed and locked after every attachment. Never use a carabiner with the gate open or partially engaged. Before trusting the attachment, pull-test the connection.

**Q: Can I mix and match Crux accessories?**
> Yes. The Crux system is modular — all accessories use the same attachment points and can be swapped or combined.
