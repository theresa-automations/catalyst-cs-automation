## Policy Summary

All Catalyst products purchased from catalystcase.com, catalystlifestyle.com, or authorized retailers come with a 12-month limited warranty from the purchase date.

---

## Key Facts (Locked — Do Not Deviate)

| Policy Point | Detail |
|---|---|
| Warranty period | 12 months from purchase date |
| Eligible purchases | catalystcase.com, catalystlifestyle.com, or authorized retailers |
| Coverage | Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship |
| Replacement warranty | 3 months from replacement shipment date |
| Resolution options | Replacement / Store credit / Refund to original payment |
| Shipping fee | Applies to replacement shipments (customer may need to cover) |
| Photo requirement | Required — front, back, top, bottom + original packaging |
| Claim form | https://forms.fillout.com/t/8jPGm4Ck6Xus |
| Review timeline | 24–48 hours after complete submission; 5–7 business days for complex claims |

---

## What IS Covered

- Manufacturing defects (broken hinge, cracked material, faulty seal, etc.)
- Material defects (premature wear and tear not caused by misuse)
- Products that malfunction due to manufacturing issues (e.g., leaky waterproof case, functional failure)
- Products that arrived already defective (factory defect)

---

## What is NOT Covered

- Accidental damage (drops, impacts, scratches)
- Damage from improper use (e.g., using non-waterproof case underwater)
- Normal wear and tear from regular use
- Unauthorized modifications or third-party repairs
- Cosmetic issues: yellowing of TPU, surface scratches from regular use
- Claims made after the 12-month warranty period

---

## Warranty Claim Process

**Step 1: Customer submits claim**
- Via warranty form: https://forms.fillout.com/t/8jPGm4Ck6Xus
- Required documentation:
  - Clear photos: front, back, top, bottom of product
  - Close-up of defect
  - Original packaging (if available)
  - Proof of purchase (order confirmation or screenshot with purchase date/amount)
  - Description of defect: when noticed, what it looks like, how it affects use

**Step 2: Review**
- Support team reviews submission within 24–48 hours
- May request additional photos or information

**Step 3: Decision**
- Approved: customer chooses resolution option
- Denied: reason explained, alternatives offered (discount, repair options)

**Step 4: Resolution**
- Replacement: new item shipped (with 3-month warranty); shipping fee may apply
- Refund: to original payment method
- Store credit: issued to account

## Exact Language to Use

**For warranty coverage explanation:**
> "All Catalyst products come with a 12-month limited warranty from the purchase date. This covers defects in materials and workmanship — meaning manufacturing issues, not accidental damage or normal wear."

**For warranty claim initiation:**
> "To start your warranty claim, please complete our form at https://forms.fillout.com/t/8jPGm4Ck6Xus. You'll need clear photos of the defect (front, back, top, bottom, plus a close-up), your order number, and your purchase date."

**For denied claims (out of warranty, <2 years old):**
> "Your product is outside the 12-month warranty period. While we can't process a full warranty claim, we'd like to offer you [X]% off a replacement to make things right."

**For denied claims (accidental damage):**
> "After reviewing your photos, the damage appears consistent with impact damage rather than a manufacturing defect. Our warranty covers manufacturing issues only. However, we'd like to help — here are some options: [alternatives]."

---

## What NOT to Say

- ❌ Never promise warranty coverage before reviewing photos
- ❌ Never approve claims for yellowing — it is cosmetic, not a manufacturing defect
- ❌ Never approve claims for damage from drops or misuse
- ❌ Never state the warranty period as anything other than 12 months
- ❌ Never say replacement warranty is 12 months — it is 3 months from replacement shipment
- ❌ Never process claims without photos
- ❌ Never make exceptions without management approval
- ❌ Never state shipping is free for replacements without verifying current policy
