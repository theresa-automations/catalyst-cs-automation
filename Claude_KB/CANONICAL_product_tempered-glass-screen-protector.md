## Installation Guide (Bubble-Free)

**Preparation:**
1. Clean the screen with the included wet wipe (alcohol) — wipe in one direction
2. Follow immediately with the dry microfiber cloth — wipe in one direction
3. Use the dust removal sticker (blue tab) to lift any remaining dust particles
4. Ensure the screen is completely clean and dust-free before proceeding

**Alignment:**
1. Place your protective case on the iPhone — this serves as the alignment frame
2. Position the screen protector inside the case opening to check alignment before peeling
3. The protector should sit inside the case edge without overlap

**Application:**
1. Peel the backing from the screen protector
2. Align carefully using the case as a guide
3. Lower the protector onto the screen from one end
4. Press down gently — it should self-adhere

**Bubble removal:**
- Use the squeegee card to push any bubbles toward the edges
- Use the dust removal sticker to lift and re-apply if a dust particle is trapped
- Small bubbles at edges may disappear on their own within 24–48 hours

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: Will this work with my protective case?**
> Yes. The screen protector uses a case-friendly design — it is slightly smaller than the full screen and does not extend to the screen edge. This prevents interference with raised-edge protective cases.

**Q: Why doesn't it include an installation tray?**
> The case-friendly design means the screen protector sits inside the case's opening, which naturally acts as an alignment guide. Your existing protective case provides better alignment than a generic tray would.

**Q: I have bubbles after installation — how do I fix them?**
> Use the included squeegee card to push bubbles toward the edges. If a bubble is caused by a dust particle, use the dust removal sticker to gently lift the affected area, remove the particle, and re-adhere. Small edge bubbles often disappear within 24–48 hours.

**Q: Can I clean it with alcohol?**
> Yes. 70% isopropyl alcohol is safe for cleaning and will not damage the protector.

**Q: What's special about the iPhone 17 version?**
> The iPhone 17 Series screen protector includes an antimicrobial coating that reduces bacteria on the surface by up to 99.9%. This is specific to the iPhone 17 version.

**Q: Is it compatible with Face ID and Touch ID?**
> Yes. The screen protector is designed for full functionality including Face ID (for models using Face ID) and does not block any sensors.
