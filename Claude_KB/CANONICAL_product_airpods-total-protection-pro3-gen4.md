## Installation Guide

1. Clean the AirPods case exterior with a soft cloth (remove any dust or debris)
2. Pull the fabric tab to open the Catalyst case
3. Place the AirPods charging case into the silicone-lined interior
4. Close the lid and engage the locking latch until it clicks
5. Verify: latch is fully closed, case opens smoothly with the fabric tab

**Opening the case:**
- Pull the fabric pull tab — do not force the latch
- One-handed operation is possible once familiar with the latch mechanism

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: What AirPods models fit this case?**
> This case fits AirPods Pro (3rd Generation) and AirPods 4 — both the standard version and the ANC version. It does NOT fit AirPods Pro 2nd Gen or AirPods 3. For those models, see the separate Catalyst case for AirPods Pro 2 and AirPods 3.

**Q: Is it safe to use underwater?**
> Yes. The case is IP68 certified to 100 meters (330 feet). It is safe for swimming, snorkeling, and water sports. Note: AirPods themselves cannot play audio underwater — the case protects the charging case while not in use.

**Q: How deep can it go?**
> Certified to 100 meters (330 feet) — this exceeds recreational scuba diving limits. The '6' in IP68 means complete dust-tight sealing in addition to waterproofing.

**Q: Can I use it in a hot tub?**
> Brief exposure to hot tubs is acceptable. Avoid prolonged exposure to temperatures above 40°C (104°F) as extreme heat can affect the seal over time.

**Q: Is it safe in cold/snowy conditions?**
> Yes. The case is compatible with snow, ice, and freezing temperatures.

**Q: Does wireless charging still work through the case?**
> Yes. The case is compatible with Qi and flat MagSafe chargers. For curved MagSafe pucks, manual positioning may be required for a good connection.

**Q: Does it slow down charging?**
> No. Charging speed is identical to the bare AirPods case — no efficiency loss.

**Q: How does the pairing button work?**
> An external tactical button on the case presses the internal AirPods pairing button without opening the case, maintaining the waterproof seal.

**Q: Does FindMy work?**
> FindMy works with AirPods Pro 3 and AirPods 4 ANC, both of which have the speaker hardware required for FindMy precision alerts. Standard AirPods 4 does not have this speaker hardware and therefore does not support FindMy precision finding — this is an Apple hardware limitation, not a case limitation.

**Q: Will my AirPods survive a drop if inside the case?**
> Yes. The dual-layer construction distributes impact forces. The case is MIL-STD-810G certified to drops of 6 meters (20 feet) onto concrete.

**Q: How durable is the latch?**
> The latch has been tested to 5,000+ open/close cycles. It uses a locking mechanism, not a simple snap — this prevents accidental opening during drops.

**Q: How does the hinge compare to competitors?**
> The hinge is integrated into the polycarbonate body — it is not an exposed metal hinge. This prevents corrosion and provides greater long-term durability.

**Q: How big does the case get with the Catalyst case on?**
> The case adds approximately 8–10mm per dimension. It still fits in pockets. The included carabiner allows it to clip to bags, belt loops, or gear.

**Q: How does the glow-in-dark feature work?**
> The photoluminescent material absorbs light and emits a soft green glow. Charge it under any light source for 10–30 minutes for best visibility in the dark.

**Q: How do I clean it?**
> Mild soap and water is sufficient for routine cleaning. For disinfecting, use EPA-approved products: 70% isopropyl alcohol, 70% ethanol, Lysol Wipes, Clorox Wipes, or OptiCide 3 Wipes.
