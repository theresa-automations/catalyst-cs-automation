## Overview

This reference is used when a customer needs help identifying their iPhone model to ensure they purchase the correct Catalyst case. Always use this guide before recommending a product — wrong model = wrong case.

---

## 5 Methods to Identify iPhone Model

**Method 1 — Settings (Most Reliable)**
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **General**
3. Tap **About**
4. Look for **Model Number** — starts with "A" followed by 4 digits (e.g., A3258)
5. Match to the model number table below

**Method 2 — Back of iPhone (Older Models Only)**
- On iPhones up to iPhone 8, the model number is printed on the back of the device in small text
- Not available on iPhone X and later

**Method 3 — Physical Features**
Compare against:
- Camera layout (single, dual, triple lens; placement)
- Screen size
- Home button vs. no Home button
- Dynamic Island vs. notch vs. no notch

**Method 4 — IMEI Lookup**
1. Dial **\*#06#** to display IMEI, OR
2. Go to Settings > General > About > IMEI
3. Visit Apple's official support page and enter the IMEI/serial number

**Method 5 — Apple's Official Model Number List**
Cross-reference with Apple's support page: support.apple.com/en-us/111900

## Why This Matters for CS

- **Case compatibility**: Each Catalyst case is model-specific — a case for iPhone 16 Pro will not fit iPhone 16 Pro Max
- **Feature confirmation**: Some features (Camera Control, Dynamic Island) are model-specific
- **Warranty verification**: Purchase date + model confirmation helps validate warranty eligibility
- **Accessory compatibility**: Screen protectors, Crux accessories, and lanyards may be model-specific

## What NOT to Say

- ❌ Never assume a customer's model from context alone — always ask them to verify
- ❌ Never recommend a case without confirming the exact iPhone model (not just "iPhone 16")
- ❌ Never state model numbers from memory without cross-referencing this table — regional variants exist
