## Product Overview

The Catalyst Influence Case with Thermo-Flow™ is a premium dual-layer protective case for **iPhone 17 Pro and iPhone 17 Pro Max only**. It combines a rigid polycarbonate (PC) backplate with a flexible TPU bumper, and features Thermo-Flow™ corner vents for heat dissipation during charging.

**This case is NOT waterproof.** It does not affect the iPhone 17 Pro's native IP68 rating.

## Installation Guide

**Before installing:**
1. Clean the iPhone with 70% isopropyl alcohol wipes and a soft cloth
2. Attach any lanyard or Crux accessories BEFORE installing the phone (easier access)

**Installation steps:**
1. Hold the case open and insert the phone **volume-button side first**
2. Align all buttons and the Camera Control button carefully
3. Press firmly around all edges until you hear/feel a distinct snap at each corner
4. Verify fit: no gaps along edges, buttons are responsive, Camera Control is flush

**Removal:**
1. Pop out a **bottom corner** first — do NOT pry from the top or sides
2. Work around the bottom edge and lift the phone out
3. Avoid using sharp objects — fingernails at corners are sufficient

## Care & Maintenance

**Routine cleaning:**
- Soft cloth with mild soap and water
- 70% isopropyl alcohol wipes (safe for disinfecting)
- EPA-approved disinfectants acceptable

**Thermo-Flow™ vent care:**
- Clear vents every few months using a Q-tip, soft brush, or compressed air
- Vents are at the corners — dust buildup reduces thermal performance

**Avoid:**
- Bleach, acetone, solvents, abrasive pads
- Sunscreen, oils, DEET, adhesive removers (degrade TPU material)
- Prolonged UV/sun exposure (causes yellowing)
- Extreme temperatures

**Recovery if exposed to chemicals:**
- Wash immediately with mild soap and water
- Follow with 70% isopropyl alcohol wipe to displace residue

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: Is the Influence Case waterproof?**
> No. The Influence Case is not waterproof and is not rated for water submersion. It does not reduce or affect the iPhone 17 Pro's native IP68 water resistance rating.

**Q: My phone feels warm while charging — is something wrong?**
> No. Warmth during charging is normal and expected. The Thermo-Flow™ vents are actively dissipating heat — this means the system is working as designed. If the phone feels excessively hot or charging stops, check for debris in the vents.

**Q: How do I clean the Thermo-Flow™ vents?**
> Use a Q-tip, soft brush, or compressed air to clear the corner vents every few months. Avoid inserting anything sharp into the vent openings.

**Q: Is the case MagSafe compatible?**
> Yes. The Influence Case has embedded neodymium magnets and is fully compatible with all MagSafe accessories and chargers. If your MagSafe charger isn't connecting firmly, remove and reinstall the case, then clean both surfaces.

**Q: Does the Camera Control button still work through the case?**
> Yes. The Camera Control pass-through is made with 9H scratch-resistant glass and supports full capacitive touch. It may feel slightly different from the bare phone — this is normal.

**Q: My case is yellowing — is this a defect?**
> Yellowing of the clear TPU is a cosmetic issue caused by UV exposure, body oils, and dyes. It does not affect the protective performance of the case and is not covered by the warranty as it is not a manufacturing defect. Minimize sun exposure and avoid contact with oils and sunscreen to slow this process.

**Q: The TPU bumper is separating from the PC backplate — is this a defect?**
> Minor separation between the TPU and PC layers after extended use is normal wear and does not reduce the case's drop protection. If separation occurs early (within warranty period) and affects function, contact cs@catalystcase.com.

**Q: Does the case work with wireless charging?**
> Yes. The case is fully compatible with all Qi and MagSafe wireless chargers. Thermo-Flow™ vents actually help dissipate heat generated during wireless charging.

**Q: What is True Sound Acoustics?**
> True Sound Acoustics redirects audio from the speaker grille to the front of the device, reducing echo and improving call clarity and speaker volume compared to using the phone without a case.

**Q: How does the lanyard attach?**
> The lanyard threads through one of four corner attachment points. These points are 10kg pull-tested. Attach the lanyard before installing the phone for easier threading. Use the included paperclip hook or equivalent tool to thread the cord.

**Q: Is the case compatible with Crux accessories?**
> Yes. The Influence Case is fully compatible with all Catalyst Crux System accessories including shoulder straps, carabiners, and ring stands. All four corner attachment points are functional.

**Q: The phone wobbles inside the case — is that normal?**
> No. The phone should sit completely flat with no wobble. Wobble indicates the case is not fully seated. Remove the phone and re-install, ensuring all four corners snap fully into place.

**Q: Can I use a screen protector with this case?**
> Yes. Use a case-friendly (flat/flush-edge) screen protector. The case has a raised lip around the screen to protect it. Incompatible screen protectors with thick edges may cause lifting.
