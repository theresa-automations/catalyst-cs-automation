## Core Technologies

### Thermo-Flow™
- **What it is:** Corner vent system that dissipates heat generated during charging
- **Where it appears:** Influence Case (iPhone 17 Pro/Pro Max)
- **Key message:** Warmth during charging is normal — Thermo-Flow is working as designed
- **Do NOT say:** "Prevents overheating" — say "helps dissipate heat"

### True Sound Acoustics™
- **What it is:** Audio redirection technology that channels sound from speaker grille toward the user
- **Effect:** Reduces echo, improves call clarity, increases perceived speaker volume
- **Where it appears:** Influence Case
- **Key message:** Customers may notice calls sound clearer with the case on than off

### MagSafe Optimization
- **What it is:** Embedded neodymium magnets aligned to Apple's MagSafe specification
- **Effect:** Strong, secure connection with all MagSafe accessories and chargers
- **Where it appears:** Influence Case, AirPods cases (Qi/MagSafe charging)
- **Key message:** Full MagSafe compatibility — no reduction in charging speed or accessory function

### Military-Grade Drop Protection
- **Standard:** MIL-STD-810G
- **What it means:** Tested and certified to survive drops under U.S. military test standards
- **Where it appears:** AirPods Total Protection Cases (certified to 6 meters / 20 feet)
- **Do NOT say:** "Military-grade" without specifying the standard (MIL-STD-810G) and tested drop height

### IP68 Waterproof Rating
- **What it means:** Certified submersion to a specified depth — the '6' means dust-tight, the '8' means waterproof beyond 1 meter
- **Catalyst standard:** IP68 to 100 meters (330 feet) for AirPods cases; iPad waterproof cases have separate ratings
- **Where it appears:** AirPods Total Protection Cases; iPad Waterproof Cases
- **Do NOT say:** The Influence Case is IP68 — it is NOT waterproof

## Brand Positioning

**Tagline:** Engineering Excellence in Device Protection

**Core differentiators vs. competitors:**
1. IP68 to 100M (vs. competitors at 1–3M)
2. MIL-STD-810G certified drops (vs. unverified marketing claims)
3. 5,000+ cycle latch testing (AirPods cases)
4. Integrated hinge design (vs. exposed metal hinges that corrode)
5. Purpose-built accessories ecosystem (Crux System)

---

## Tone Guidance for CS (Brand Voice)

**Use:**
- Confident, specific, engineering-led language
- Specific numbers and certifications (IP68, MIL-STD-810G, 100M, 5,000 cycles)
- Solution-focused, warm, premium brand tone

**Avoid:**
- Vague superlatives ("best protection," "top of the line") without backing
- Overpromising ("will never break," "completely indestructible")
- Downplaying customer concerns ("that's normal, don't worry")

---

## What NOT to Say

- ❌ Never claim the Influence Case is waterproof — it is not
- ❌ Never use "military-grade" without citing MIL-STD-810G and the tested drop height
- ❌ Never claim patent protection without verifying the current patent portfolio
- ❌ Never state a waterproof depth without citing IP68 certification for that product
