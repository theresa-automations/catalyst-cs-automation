## MANDATORY: Water Test Before First Use

**This step is non-negotiable. All customers must perform this test before inserting their iPad.**

**Why:** To verify the seal is intact before entrusting an expensive device to the case.

**Procedure:**
1. Ensure the case is completely EMPTY (no iPad inside)
2. Close and snap the case shut along the entire perimeter
3. Submerge the empty case in water for **30 minutes**
4. Remove from water and dry the exterior completely
5. Open the case and inspect the interior for any moisture
6. **If dry:** Case is ready to use — proceed with iPad installation
7. **If any moisture is present:** Do NOT use the case. Contact info@catalystlifestyle.com immediately — this indicates a manufacturing defect

**When to re-test:**
- After any significant drop or impact
- If the case has been stored unused for an extended period
- Any time you suspect the seal may have been compromised

## Installation Guide

**Prepare the iPad:**
1. Clean the iPad thoroughly with a soft, lint-free cloth
2. Remove any screen protector or tempered glass — these can break the waterproof seal
3. Ensure the iPad is completely dry

**Install the iPad:**
1. Open the case using the provided opening tool (insert into the slot in the lower left corner) or a coin
2. Place the iPad face-down into the back half of the case
3. Press down firmly until you feel/hear a click — the iPad is seated
4. Close the front cover of the case
5. Align the case so the Touch ID button and camera opening are correctly positioned
6. Starting from a corner, press and snap the case closed around the entire perimeter
7. Work your way around — every snap point must be fully engaged

**Verify correct installation:**
- No gaps visible anywhere around the perimeter
- Touch ID opening is aligned with the Home button
- Camera opening is aligned with the rear camera
- Touchscreen responds normally (see troubleshooting below if not)

---

## Removal Guide

1. Insert the opening tool (or a coin) into the designated slot at the **lower left corner**
2. Gently lever to disengage the snap at that corner
3. Work around the perimeter, disengaging snaps one by one
4. **DO NOT** peel the front cover back like a "banana peel" — this can permanently damage the waterproof seal
5. Once all snaps are released, press thumbs on the back surface of the iPad to push it out

## Care & Maintenance

**Routine cleaning:**
- Soft cloth with mild soap and water
- 70% isopropyl alcohol wipes (safe for disinfecting)

**After water/saltwater exposure:**
- Rinse the exterior with fresh water
- Allow to fully dry before re-sealing

**Temperature:**
- Operating range: 32°F–95°F (0°C–35°C)
- Avoid leaving in hot cars or direct sun for extended periods

**Avoid:**
- Bleach, acetone, harsh solvents

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: Do I really need to do the water test?**
> Yes, absolutely. This is mandatory before first use. The water test verifies the seal is intact before you trust it with your iPad. Submerse the empty case for 30 minutes, then check the interior for moisture. If any moisture is present, do not use the case — contact support.

**Q: My touchscreen is acting laggy or unresponsive after installing — what's wrong?**
> This is a known pressure imbalance issue. The sealed case creates a slight air pressure difference that can affect touch sensitivity. Solution: open the charging port plug (or any port opening) briefly to equalize the air pressure. This typically resolves the issue immediately.

**Q: The water test showed moisture inside — what do I do?**
> Do not use the case. This indicates a manufacturing defect. Contact info@catalystlifestyle.com immediately with your order number and a photo of the moisture. This is covered under the 12-month warranty.

**Q: How do I open the case?**
> Use the opening tool included in the kit, or a coin. Insert it into the designated slot at the lower left corner and gently lever the snap open. Then work around the perimeter. Do not peel the front cover back — this damages the seal.

**Q: What happens if I drop the case while my iPad is inside?**
> After any significant drop, perform the water test again (with the iPad removed) before relying on the waterproofing. Impact can sometimes affect the seal integrity.

**Q: Can I use a screen protector inside the case?**
> No. Remove all screen protectors before installation. A screen protector prevents proper seating of the iPad inside the case and can break the waterproof seal.

**Q: What is the operating temperature range?**
> 32°F to 95°F (0°C to 35°C). Avoid exposing the case to temperatures outside this range for extended periods.

**Q: How do I clean the case?**
> Mild soap and water with a soft cloth for routine cleaning. 70% isopropyl alcohol wipes are safe for disinfecting. Avoid bleach and solvents.
