## Policy Summary

Catalyst accepts returns and exchanges on direct purchases from catalystcase.com and catalystlifestyle.com within 30 days of purchase. Third-party purchases (Amazon, Best Buy, resellers) must use the platform's own return policy.

---

## Key Facts (Locked — Do Not Deviate)

| Policy Point | Detail |
|---|---|
| Return window | 30 days from purchase date |
| Eligible purchases | Direct from catalystcase.com or catalystlifestyle.com ONLY |
| Third-party purchases | NOT eligible — customer must use Amazon/Best Buy/reseller policy |
| Item condition required | New condition, original packaging, all accessories included |

## Return Process — Step by Step

**Step 1: Customer initiates return**
- US: Log in at https://catalystcase.com/pages/returns
- International: Log in at https://catalystlifestyle.com/pages/returns
- Navigate to "My Orders" and submit a return request
- Return window: 30 days from purchase

**Step 2: Photo submission (REQUIRED)**
- Customer must submit photos BEFORE shipping the item back
- Required photos: front, back, top, bottom of product + original packaging
- Returns cannot proceed without photo approval

**Step 3: Approval and RMA**
- Support team reviews the request and photos
- If approved: customer receives RMA number and shipping instructions
- RMA number must be written on the outside of the return package
- RMA is valid for 15 days — customer must ship within this window

**Step 4: Customer ships item**
- Customer is responsible for return shipping costs
- Customer must provide tracking number to support team

**Step 5: Resolution**
- Upon receipt, item is inspected
- Refund processed within 5–10 business days to original payment method
- Or: exchange shipped / store credit issued per customer's choice

---

## Resolution Options

**Exact language to use:**
> "Once we receive and inspect your return, you can choose from: an exchange for an equal-value item, store credit for a future purchase, or a refund to your original payment method. Please note that the original shipping fee is non-refundable, and a 5% restocking fee applies."

**Exchange:**
- For equal-value item only
- New item ships once return is received and inspected

**Store Credit:**
- Issued to account — no expiration [verify if expiration applies]
- Full value minus restocking fee

**Refund:**
- To original payment method only
- 5–10 business days to appear
- Minus restocking fee (5% standard; 15% if missing parts/packaging)
- Original shipping cost is NOT refunded

---

## Eligibility Rules

**Eligible:**
- Direct purchase from catalystcase.com or catalystlifestyle.com
- Within 30 days of purchase
- Item in new/unused condition
- Original packaging intact
- All accessories included

**Not eligible:**
- Purchased from Amazon, Best Buy, or any third-party reseller → redirect to that platform
- Outside the 30-day window
- Item shows signs of use, damage, or modification
- Missing original packaging or accessories (may be subject to 15% fee or return-to-sender)
- No photos submitted

| Missing photos | Request photos before processing RMA |
| Missing packaging | Advise 15% restocking fee or possible return-to-sender |

---

## What NOT to Say

- ❌ Never process returns for Amazon, Best Buy, or reseller purchases
- ❌ Never skip the photo submission requirement — photos are mandatory
- ❌ Never issue an RMA without photo approval
- ❌ Never promise a refund timeline shorter than 5–10 business days
- ❌ Never waive the restocking fee without management approval
- ❌ Never state return window as anything other than 30 days from purchase
- ❌ Never say shipping fees are refundable — they are not
- ❌ Never make exceptions to the "new condition" requirement without escalating
