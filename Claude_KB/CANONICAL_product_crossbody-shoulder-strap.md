## Product Overview

The Catalyst Crossbody Shoulder Strap is a fully adjustable shoulder strap designed to attach to compatible phone cases. It works with Catalyst Impact Cases and any non-Catalyst case that has dedicated attachment points (small loops or rings).

## Assembly & Installation Guide

**Step 1: Locate attachment points on the case**
- Attachment points are the small loops or rings on the corners of compatible cases
- Catalyst Impact Cases have dedicated attachment points built in

**Step 2: Connect the plastic connectors**
- Thread each plastic connector through an attachment point on the case
- Ensure the connector is fully seated and cannot slip out

**Step 3: Attach the shoulder strap**
- Clip the circular carabiners to the plastic connectors
- Ensure each carabiner gate is fully closed

**Step 4: Verify security**
- Give the strap a gentle but firm tug to confirm all connections hold
- This step is CRITICAL — do not skip it

---

## Strap Adjustment Guide

1. Thread the strap through the adjustable slider to your desired length
2. **Immediately after adjusting: slide the adjuster close to one of the connectors** — this is the security lock position
3. A slider left in the middle of the strap can allow the strap to extend unexpectedly
4. To shorten: push the slider toward a connector while pulling the excess strap through
5. To lengthen: pull the strap through the slider away from a connector

## Care & Maintenance

- **Washing:** Hand wash only in cold water with mild soap
- **Drying:** Air dry completely — do not machine dry or tumble dry
- **Do not:** Machine wash, use harsh detergents, bleach, or hot water
- **Inspect regularly:** Check plastic connectors and carabiner gates for wear before each use

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: How do I make sure the strap won't come loose?**
> After clipping in, give the strap a firm tug to test all connection points. Equally important: after adjusting the strap length, slide the adjuster close to one of the connectors. A loose slider can allow the strap to slip and extend. This is the most common cause of unexpected strap failure.

**Q: Will this work with my non-Catalyst case?**
> Yes, if your case has dedicated attachment points — small loops or rings designed for this purpose. The strap is not compatible with cases that have no attachment points.

**Q: How do I clean the strap?**
> Hand wash only in cold water with mild soap. Air dry completely. Do not machine wash or use hot water — this can degrade the material and stitching.

**Q: Can I use this with any Catalyst case?**
> The strap is designed for Catalyst Impact Cases that have built-in attachment points. Check your specific case model to confirm attachment points are present.
