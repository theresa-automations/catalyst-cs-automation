## Product Overview

The Catalyst Total Protection Case for AirPods Pro 2/2.1 & AirPods 3 is a waterproof, drop-proof hardshell case for AirPods charging cases. It features an IP68 rating to 100 meters and MIL-STD-810G drop protection certified to 6 meters.

**Compatible with: AirPods Pro (2nd Generation), AirPods Pro (2.1), and AirPods (3rd Generation).**
**NOT compatible with: AirPods 4, AirPods Pro 3, AirPods 2, or original AirPods.** (For AirPods Pro 3 or AirPods 4, see: CANONICAL_product_airpods-total-protection-pro3-gen4.md)

> ⚠️ COMPATIBILITY CRITICAL: AirPods 4 does NOT fit this case. Customers with AirPods 4 need the separate Pro 3 & Gen 4 case. Always confirm the customer's exact AirPods model before recommending.

## Installation Guide

1. Clean the AirPods case exterior with a soft cloth
2. Pull the fabric tab to open the Catalyst case
3. Place the AirPods charging case into the silicone-lined interior
4. Close the lid and engage the locking latch until it clicks
5. Verify: latch is fully closed, fabric tab opens the case smoothly

## FAQ — Approved Answers

**Q: What AirPods models fit this case?**
> This case fits AirPods Pro 2nd Generation (including 2.1) and AirPods 3rd Generation. It does NOT fit AirPods 4 or AirPods Pro 3. For those models, there is a separate Catalyst case — please confirm your exact model.

**Q: I have AirPods 4 — will this case work?**
> No. AirPods 4 require the Catalyst Total Protection Case for AirPods Pro 3 & AirPods 4, which is a separate product. The charging case dimensions are different and AirPods 4 will not fit properly in this case.

**Q: Is it waterproof?**
> Yes. IP68 certified to 100 meters (330 feet). Safe for swimming, snorkeling, and water sports.

**Q: Can I use it in a hot tub?**
> Brief exposure is acceptable. Avoid prolonged exposure above 40°C (104°F).

**Q: Does wireless charging work?**
> Yes — Qi and flat MagSafe. Curved MagSafe pucks require manual positioning.

**Q: How does the pairing button work?**
> An external tactical button presses the internal AirPods pairing button without opening the case, preserving the waterproof seal.

**Q: Does FindMy work?**
> FindMy works with AirPods Pro 2 models that have ANC, which includes the speaker hardware required for FindMy precision alerts. Standard AirPods 3 does not support FindMy precision finding.

**Q: How durable is the latch?**
> Tested to 5,000+ open/close cycles with a locking mechanism that prevents accidental opening on impact.

**Q: How do I clean it?**
> Mild soap and water for routine cleaning. For disinfecting: 70% isopropyl alcohol, Lysol Wipes, Clorox Wipes, or OptiCide 3 Wipes.

**Q: How does the glow-in-dark feature work?**
> The photoluminescent material charges under any light source in 10–30 minutes and emits a soft green glow in the dark.
